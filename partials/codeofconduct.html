Agile and Beyond Code of Conduct

The Agile & Beyond conference has always fostered an environment for the open exchange of ideas in a safe and respectful manner. To ensure these goals we ask that all participants are aware of and adherent to these expectations.

  • Be open, honest, and trustworthy
  • Give proper credit for information sources and intellectual property
  • If you promote a product or service provider, declare your affiliation or association
  • Avoid harm to others - physical, emotional, or otherwise
  • Refrain from harsh language or pointed humor that disparages the dignity and social equity of any individual or group
  • Respect all ethical, economic, cultural, legal, and environmental norms
  • Alert conference organizers if you notice a dangerous situation or someone in distress

Together we can maintain Agile & Beyond as a premier conference respected and appreciated by all.
