Location

The Adoba Hotel

The Adoba Hotel in Dearborn, MI (Formerly the Hyatt Regency Dearborn) will be the host to this year’s conference. With the increased space that the Adoba offers will will be able to manage the conference and give you an overall better experience.

The Adoba Hotel Dearborn is a major conference center hotel located in the Metro Detroit suburb of Dearborn, Michigan at 600 Town Center Drive (Fairlane Mall), near the intersection of Michigan State Highway 39 (Southfield Freeway) and U.S. Route 12 (Michigan Ave).

Reserving a Room at the Adoba

To reserve a room at the Adoba for $85 on either Friday or Saturday night, go to www.adobadearborn.com and at the top of the page, enter “AGILE” as the IATA code, then click “Book Now”.

About the Adoba

The mission of our host facility for this year’s conference is very similar to that of the agile community – social responsibility, conservation of resources, sustainability, and embracing change. The Adoba Eco Hotel & Suites Brand launched on the anniversary of Earth Day 2010, underscoring this new, innovative brand concept’s commitment to environmental awareness and social responsibility. The new Adoba Hotel Dearborn/Detroit is stepping up and striving to lead the way in sustainable, green hospitality. This means taking responsibility for conserving Earth’s natural resources, supporting local cultures, and making a positive impact on the footprint we leave, one guest stay at a time. The Adoba Eco Hotel brand is committed not only to eco-friendly practices, but encouraging the entire hospitality industry to embrace the change. Every traveler has the power to make a positive decision – “Travel with a Purpose and Change the World Today”. To learn more, please visit http://adobadearborn.com/
